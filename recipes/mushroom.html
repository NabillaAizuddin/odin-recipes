Mushroom Topping

Description

This is the best mushroom topping in the world. if you make this mushroom topping, everyone will be happy and wanting to eat it again.

Do make this mushroom topping. Make it a priority in your day to make this delicious mushroom topping!

Ingredients

  • Mushrooms
  • More mushrooms
  • More MORE Mushrooms

Steps

  1. Blanch mushrooms
  2. Add mushroom to a boiling water
  3. Take out the mushroom to another bowl
  4. Put the mushrooms onto the bread
